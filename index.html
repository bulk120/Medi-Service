About Our NGO

Medi Service is a registered non-governmental organization (UDYAM-AS-30-0024667) based in Hajo, Dadara, Kamrup, Assam. We focus on community upliftment through healthcare, education, and skill development.

Our mission is to build self-reliant communities by providing access to essential services, awareness programs, and sustainable livelihood opportunities.
