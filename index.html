About Our NGO

KM Edhi Business is a registered non-governmental organization (UDYAM-AS-30-0024667) based in Hajo, Dadara, Pachim Dadora, Kamrup, Assam. Founded with a vision to uplift rural communities, we focus on sustainable development through education, skill training, and economic empowerment initiatives.

Our mission is to create self-reliant communities by providing access to education, vocational training, and resources that enable individuals to build better futures for themselves and their families.

We work closely with local communities to identify needs and develop tailored programs that address specific challenges while preserving the cultural heritage of Assam.

Our Programs

Education for All

Providing access to quality education for children in remote villages, including after-school tutoring, digital literacy programs, and scholarship opportunities for deserving students.

Skill Development

Vocational training programs in tailoring, handicrafts, organic farming, and small business management to empower youth and women with marketable skills.

Healthcare & Sanitation

Organizing health camps, promoting hygiene awareness, and facilitating access to clean water and sanitation facilities in underserved communities.
